What It Does

Lesson Mentor is designed to take the guesswork out of differentiating a real lesson for a real class.

Rewrite lesson plans

Upload or paste a lesson, then generate a teacher-ready version aligned to class learning profiles and selected systems.

Launch student assessments

Release the same question set to a whole class, a group, or one new student, with read-aloud and teacher display options.

Embed IEP supports

Class-level supports are placed intentionally into the lesson, with actual sentence frames and usable teacher language.

Reduce standards stress

Common Core and NGSS matching are part of the roadmap so teachers do not have to hunt down standards manually.
